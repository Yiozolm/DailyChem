# Compound C001: ethyl benzoate

Compound C001 (ethyl benzoate) was obtained as a colorless oil.

To a solution of benzoic acid (1.0 mmol) and ethanol (5 mL) in ethanol was added sulfuric acid (catalytic). The reaction mixture was stirred at reflux for 4 h. The mixture was diluted with water and extracted with ethyl acetate. The crude product was purified by column chromatography to afford ethyl benzoate (120 mg, 63%).

## Characterization

1H NMR (400 MHz, CDCl3) δ 8.56 (s, 2H), 7.51 (s, 2H), 5.17 (s, 2H), 3.11 (s, 9H), 2.72 (s, 2H), 1.24 (s, 2H).

13C NMR (101 MHz, CDCl3) δ 165.2, 132.1, 129.5, 128.4, 52.1.

HRMS (ESI) m/z calculated for C9H10O2Na [M + Na]+ 173.0573, found 173.0570.
